1. Native Computer Vision Engine

Slouch Guard relies on Windows.Media.FaceAnalysis.FaceDetector:

  • Ultra-Low Compute: Uses under 1.5% CPU and 45MB RAM.
  • 100% In-Memory Processing: Frames are processed transiently in RAM.

2. Triple-Vector Posture Detection

Combines three mathematical vectors:

  • Vertical Position (Y-Axis): Detects forward head posture.
  • Face Bounding Area: Calculates distance changes relative to your screen.
