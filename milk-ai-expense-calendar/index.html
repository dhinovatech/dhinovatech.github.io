Core Features & Innovations

Why Milk AI Calendar Stands Out

1. Gemini AI Voice Engine

Hands-free natural language voice logging powered by GeminiApiClient.kt. Elderly family members and busy homemakers can simply tap the mic and speak:

  • Multi-Dialect Natural Commands: Speak in English or Hindi (e.g. "Today no milk", "From 5th to 12th make it 2 liters", "Yesterday morning 1L and evening 1.5L").
  • Intelligent Intent Parsing: Automatically extracts dates, morning/evening shift types, liter quantities, and price overrides into structured records.
  • Live Transcribe & Edit Modal: Inspect and confirm transcribed data before saving to ensure 100% precision.

2. Interactive Monthly Calendar

Designed in MonthlyScreen.kt with Jetpack Compose & Material Design 3 for instant visual clarity:

  • Color-Coded Status Accents: Green accent indicates confirmed milk delivery; Red accent highlights skipped or zero-quantity days.
  • Dual Shift Support (Morning & Evening): Built specifically for Indian households purchasing different milk types (e.g. cow milk in the morning, buffalo milk in the evening).
  • Quick Increment Controls: Fast + / - adjustments (0.1L, 0.25L, 0.5L, 1L) for rapid manual entry.

1-Tap WhatsApp Invoicing

Generates itemized PDF invoices, CSV spreadsheets, and high-res monthly calendar snapshots via ShareHelper.kt for instant WhatsApp sharing with your milk vendor.

12+ Indian Languages

Full localization for English, Hindi (हिन्दी), Bengali (বাংলা), Telugu (తెలుగు), Tamil (தமிழ்), Marathi (मराठी), Gujarati (ગુજરાતી), Kannada, Malayalam, Odia, Punjabi, and Urdu.

100% Local Privacy & Backup

Offline-first Room SQLite storage keeps all logs strictly on device. Includes JSON export/import (BackupHelper.kt) for seamless phone migrations.
