Notelock

Secure Your Thoughts with Very High AES-256 Encryption

Notelock is a privacy-focused text editor designed to keep your personal notes, passwords, and sensitive information safe from prying eyes. Fortified with AES-256 encryption, unique cryptographic salts, and custom .dhin extension support.

Why Write with Notelock?

AES-256 High Security

Every note file is scrambled with AES-256 encryption, unique cryptographic salts, and initialization vectors (IV) for every single save.

Zero-Knowledge Privacy

Runs entirely offline on your computer. Passwords and decrypted texts never touch cloud servers. You hold the only key.

File Explorer Integration

Open and save encrypted .dhin files directly through Windows File Explorer and native system dialogs.

How Notelock Operates

01
Write

Type your notes, passwords, ideas, or financial records in a clean distraction-free editor.

02
Lock (.dhin)

Hit Save, enter your master password, and your content is instantly encrypted into a .dhin file.

03
Unlock

Double-click your .dhin file, enter your password, and view decrypted text instantly.
